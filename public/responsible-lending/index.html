Responsible lending

Secure operates under responsible lending principles in Belgium. We aim to prevent over-indebtedness and misuse of collateralized assets.

Our commitments

  • No “guaranteed approval”. Every file is reviewed by humans.
  • Affordability checks using declared income/activity and existing obligations.
  • Transparent APRs, fees, repayment schedules and consequences of default.
  • Right to repay early without penalties on interest not accrued.
  • Clear handling of pledged assets: secure custody, insurance, and transparent liquidation steps if needed.

When we decline

If affordability or risk thresholds are not met, or the collateral is not suitable/legal, we will decline the request and explain why when possible.

Support and complaints

Contact hello@secure.oryxen.tech for assistance. You may escalate to Belgian consumer protection authorities if needed.

Updates

This statement may be updated to reflect regulation or internal controls. Significant changes will be communicated in-app.
